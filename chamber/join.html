  • All Bronze benefits
  • Priority listing in the business directory
  • Featured spot in chamber newsletters
  • Invitations to exclusive networking events

Benefits of Gold membership

  • All Silver benefits
  • Premium advertising opportunities
  • Personalized business support and consulting
  • Sponsorship opportunities at major events
