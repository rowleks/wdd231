BookHive – Online Bookstore Site Plan

Site Name

BookHive – An inviting, memorable name for an online bookstore that reflects a cozy community for readers and book lovers.

Site Purpose

The site showcases curated book collections, allows customers to browse by genre, place orders for physical or digital books, and save favorite titles to a personal wishlist. It's designed to be responsive and user-friendly on both desktop and mobile devices.

Scenarios

  • “I want to quickly find new releases and staff picks in my favorite genre.”
  • “Can I place an order for home delivery or buy an ebook from my phone?”

Color Scheme

  • Primary: #3E2C24 (warm coffee brown — header/footer, primary buttons, key headings)
  • Secondary: #F6F3EE (paper off-white — page and card backgrounds)
  • Accent: #AEC785 (sage green — CTAs, links, badges, hover states)
  • Text: #222222 (deep charcoal — body text, captions, metadata)

Typography

  • Headings: Merriweather (Serif)
  • Body: Roboto (Sans-serif)
